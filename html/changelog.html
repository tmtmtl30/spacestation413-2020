16 November 2020

ArcaneDefence updated:

  • Used first aid and hypovolemic autoinjectors aren't invisible any more
  • Things made of edible materials are more consistently edible, and can no longer be eaten multiple times over by spam clicking them. One serving per pizza floor only, please.

ArcaneMusic updated:

  • Snail's shells, if removed from their body, are now destroyed as appropriate.

Qustinnus updated:

  • Movement now works even if client dir is changed

uomo91 updated:

  • Fixed "Show All" tab in player panel logs being broken.
  • Whispers display differently in logs, distinguishing them from say logs in display.
  • Player panel logs will now show all logs chronologically, so you'll see commingled say and attack logs if you're on the "Show All" tab, etc...

15 November 2020

ArcaneDefence updated:

  • Condiments work with food items more consistently, and packets don't give useless feedback.

ArcaneMusic updated:

  • Vending Uis work once again with no ID/viewing as a ghost.
  • Rats and Regal Rats now properly trigger mouse traps when crossed.
  • Vending machines now properly apply inflation across the market crash event.

Fikou updated:

  • adds a training machine to delta firing range

14 November 2020

ATHATH updated:

  • The shocks from the shock touch mutation now ignore insulated gloves.
  • The shocks from punching or running into charged energy fields now DON'T ignore insulated gloves.
  • The shocks from liquid electricity now ignore insulated gloves.

ArcaneMusic updated:

  • The Medical NanoMed vending machine has been split into two, with the all new NanoDrug vending machine taking most chemical medicines.
  • Literally everything purchasable in a vending machine has had it's prices adjusted to better suit players to buy more things, while still enabling them to interact with each other using money.
  • Player passive income has been removed.
  • Departmental budgets income has been thoroughly reworked, as they no longer need to balance the whole of the crew's income off of them, making them a more reasonable resource pool scaled to the station's size or efforts.
  • Departmental income is a static value for every department, getting more funds every 5 minutes (Plus whatever's added from vending machines!)
  • Buying goods through your department's vendors now provides an 80% discount as opposed to being completely free.
  • Butchering mobs with embedded objects inside of them will now drop the embedded objects as well.

Cartographer-D updated:

  • fixed the meatball emoji

EOBGames updated:

  • Heretic has received a minor textual facelift.

IndieanaJones updated:

  • Fixed Space Ninja's console hack occasionally doing nothing.

UnderARock updated:

  • Hardcore random characters will now receive points for surviving.

itseasytosee updated:

  • fixed some intentional garbling in a hijacking message

nightred updated:

  • Stomachs no longer metabolize reagents, they process them to the body

  • This hopefully fixes the whitescreen problem.
